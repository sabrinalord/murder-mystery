The Characters

The General

A retired army general, he's pompous and a show off - he likes a joke as long as it's not on him.

Lady Lord

Arrogant, spoilt and charming when she wants to be. Lady Lord is the General's much younger second wife.

The Brother

Game hunter and younger brother to the General. Almost as pompous and not nearly as successful - he's been in his brother's shadow all his life.

The Son

The eldest son of the general, a writer and a sensitive soul. He's just back from fighting in the war and he's got the scars to prove it.

The Daughter

A glamourous champion of women's rights. She loves Greta Garbo and hates it when she doesn't get her own way. She thinks that revenge is best served in style.

The Landlady

A class act she owns the Bodega speakeasy with her husband and partner in crime. She's a no-nonsense, ruthless ruffian who always stands up for her friends.

The Landlord

Owner of the Bodega club. An impeccable criminal and hardened gangster, he always gets what he wants one way or another.

The Dancer

A whirlwind of feathers and sequins, she works at the Bodega club. She could dazzle anyone with her smile.

The Poker Player

A Slick Rick and the best card player at the Bodega. Nobody can tell when he's bluffing. He's an incessant gambler who will bleed you dry if you're not careful.

The Accountant

Clever and seemingly sensible, but can they be trusted? The Accountant been looking after the General's finances for years. You could say he or she is heavily invested in the welfare of the estate.

The Chef

Unpredictable and rebellious, the Chef has been waiting for a day off for months. If only the General would give him/her the time of day to notice.

The Vicar

Vicar of the local parish church, a man of faith - some would say he's been looking a little jumpy lately, especially when the general is near by...

The Clairvoyant

Best friend to Lady Lord. A well known mystic and medium. She's as famous for her dramatics as she is for her psychic abilities.

The Doctor

Friend and confidant of the General's late, former wife. The doctor know's the ins and outs of each family member... but where does his or her loyalties lie?

The Maid

A skilled herbalist she saved lives during the war, now she has to put up with the General's violent temper. She's seen and heard everything, but does she know too much?

HOW TO PLAY

  • Choose your character before or on the night
  • When you arrive you'll each get an envelope with information and a secret task.
  • Act as much or as little as you like but try to stay in character.
  • The game will begin when the clock strikes.

Getting here

  • Get to Harrietsham station for 4pm (before it gets dark)
  • The house is a bit hard to find and there are no street lights.
  • At Harrietsham station, go to platform 2, there is a dirt footpath.
