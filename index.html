The Characters

The General

A retired army general, he's pompous and a show off - he likes a joke as long as it's not on him.

Lady Lord

People are always saying 'She's too young for him', Lady Lord is the austere mistress turned second wife of the general.

The Brother

Game hunter and younger brother to the General. Almost as pompous and not nearly as successful - he's been in his brother's shadow all his life.

The Son

The eldest son of the general, a writer and a sensitive soul. He's just back from fighting in the war and he's got the scars to prove it.

The Daughter

A glamourous champion of women's rights. She loves Greta Garbo and hates it when she doesn't get her own way. She thinks that revenge is best served in style.

The Landlady

A class act she owns the Bodega speakeasy with her husband and partner in crime. She's a no-nonsense, ruthless ruffian who always stands up for her friends.

The Landlord

Owner of the Bodega club, an impeccable criminal and hardened gangster, he wont let any debt go unpaid.

The Dancer

A whirlwind of feathers and sequins, she works at the Bodega club. She could dazzle anyone with her smile.

The Poker Player

A Slick Rick and the best card player at the Bodega. Nobody can tell when he's bluffing. He's an incessant gambler who will bleed you dry if you're not careful.

The Accountant

Dangerously intelligent, the General's accountant has been looking after his finances for years. You could say the accountant is heavily invested in the welfare of the estate.

The Chef

Unpredictable and rebellious, the Chef has been waiting for a day off for months. If only the General would give him/her the time of day to notice.

The Vicar

Vicar of the local parish church, a man of faith - some would say he's been looking a little jumpy lately.

The Clairvoyant

A well known mystic and medium. She's as famous for her dramatics as she is for her psychic abilities.

The Doctor

Calm natured and an obeserver of people, the doctor know's the ins and outs of each family member... but where does his or her loyalties lie?

The Maid

A skilled herbalist she saved lives during the war. Now a maid for the family, she's seen and heard everything. But does she know too much?

HOW TO PLAY

  • Choose your character before or choose from a box of items on the night.
  • Get to the house for 4pm.
  • When you arrive you'll get your item and an envelope
  • You can act as much as you want, most of it's task based
  • The murder mystery will be over by 9pm.
  • Party time afterwards, everyone is welcome to stay over.
