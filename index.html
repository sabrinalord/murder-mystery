The Characters

The General

A retired army general, pompous and a show off - he likes a joke as long as it's not on him! Every year he hosts his annual Halloween party where he likes to spook his guests.

Lady Lord

Fed up of people saying 'She's too young for him' Lady Lord is the austere mistress turned second wife of the general.

The Brother

Younger brother to the General. Almost as pompous and not nearly as successful - you've been in your brother's shadow all your life.

The Son

The eldest son of the general, a romantic and the sensible one in the family.

The Daughter

A glamourous champion of women's rights. She loves Greta Garbo and hates it when she doesn't get her own way. She thinks that revenge is best served in style.

The Landlady

You own the bodega speakeasy with your husband and partner in crime. You're a no-nonsense, ruthless ruffian who always stands up for her friends.

The Landlord

Owner of the bodega speakeasy, an impeccable criminal and hardened gangster, you wont let any debt go unpaid.

The Dancer

A whirlwind of feathers and sequins, you work at the Bodega club. You could dazzle anyone with your smile.

The Accountant

Reserved and trustworthy, you've been looking after the general's finances for years. Other's would say that on all accounts you're heavily invested in the welfare of the estate.

The Chef

When you're not stuck in the kitchen you're charasmatic and funny, if only the General would give you the time of day to notice!

The Vicar

Vicar of the local parish church, a man of faith - some would say he's been looking a little jumpy lately.

The Clairvoyant

A famous mystic and medium. You're as known for your dramatics as you are for your psychic abilities.

The Doctor

You've been the family doctor for years, you know the ins and outs of the whole family but where do your loyalties lie?

The Maid

Quiet and self-effacing you wouldn't hurt a fly... It's safe to say you've seen and heard everything, but do you know too much?

HOW TO PLAY

  • Choose your character before or choose on the night
  • Get to my house for 4/5pm - (before it gets dark - there are no street lights and house is hard to find)
  • Get your characters item and envelope
  • Act as much as you want, most of it's task based
